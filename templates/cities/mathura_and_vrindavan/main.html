Mathura is a holy city in Hinduism and is considered the heart of Brij Bhoomi, the land of Krishna. The twin-city to Mathura is Vrindavan. There are many places of historic and religious importance in Mathura and its neighbouring towns. It's home to temples, many dedicated to Krishna and his lover, the deity Radha.
